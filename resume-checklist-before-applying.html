Resume Checklist Before Applying for a Job

Before submitting your resume, it is important to review every section carefully. Small mistakes can reduce your chances of getting shortlisted.

Resume Checklist

  • Correct contact information
  • Professional email address
  • No spelling mistakes
  • ATS-friendly format
  • Relevant skills added
  • Updated work experience
  • Proper resume summary
  • Correct job keywords
  • Professional formatting
  • PDF format ready

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Using outdated information
  • Adding irrelevant details
  • Too many pages
  • Grammar mistakes

Conclusion

Following a resume checklist can improve your chances of passing ATS screening and getting interviews.
