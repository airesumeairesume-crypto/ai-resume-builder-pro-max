Resume for Receptionist Freshers

A receptionist is responsible for greeting visitors, answering phone calls, and managing front desk activities.

Important Skills

  • Communication Skills
  • Telephone Handling
  • Customer Service
  • Time Management
  • Computer Knowledge

Resume Structure

  1. Contact Information
  2. Career Objective
  3. Skills
  4. Education
  5. Internship or Training

Conclusion

A receptionist resume should demonstrate communication, professionalism, and organizational abilities.
