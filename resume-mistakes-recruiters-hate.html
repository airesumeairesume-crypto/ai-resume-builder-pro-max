What Are the Biggest Resume Mistakes That Make Recruiters Reject Candidates?

Recruiters often spend less than 10 seconds reviewing a resume. Small mistakes can lead to immediate rejection.

Common Resume Mistakes

  • Spelling and grammar errors
  • Using an unprofessional email address
  • Adding irrelevant information
  • Writing long paragraphs
  • Using graphics that ATS cannot read
  • Not including job-specific keywords
  • Adding false information

How to Avoid These Mistakes

  • Proofread carefully
  • Use an ATS-friendly template
  • Keep your resume concise
  • Customize your resume for each job

Conclusion

A professional resume can significantly increase interview opportunities.
