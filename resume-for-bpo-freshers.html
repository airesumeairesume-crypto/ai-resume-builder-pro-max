Resume for BPO Freshers

BPO companies look for candidates with communication skills, customer handling abilities, and basic computer knowledge.

Important Skills

  • Communication Skills
  • Customer Service
  • Computer Knowledge
  • Typing Skills
  • Problem Solving
  • Teamwork

Resume Structure

  1. Contact Information
  2. Career Objective
  3. Education
  4. Skills
  5. Certifications

Conclusion

A professional BPO resume can increase your chances of getting selected for interviews.
