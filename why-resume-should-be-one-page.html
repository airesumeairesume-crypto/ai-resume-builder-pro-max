Why Do Hiring Managers Suggest That Your Resume Not Exceed One Page?

Recruiters review hundreds of resumes every week. A one-page resume helps them quickly identify your qualifications.

Benefits of a One-Page Resume

  • Easy to read
  • Highlights important information
  • Looks professional
  • Saves recruiters time

When Two Pages Are Acceptable

  • 10+ years of experience
  • Technical portfolios
  • Academic CVs
