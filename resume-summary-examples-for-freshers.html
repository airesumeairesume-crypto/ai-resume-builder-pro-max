Resume Summary Examples for Freshers (2026)

A resume summary is a short introduction placed at the top of your resume. It helps recruiters quickly understand your skills, education, and career goals.

Example 1

Motivated and detail-oriented graduate seeking an entry-level position to apply problem-solving and communication skills while contributing to organizational growth.

Example 2

Recent graduate with strong academic performance and hands-on project experience. Eager to learn, grow, and contribute to a dynamic team.

Tips for Writing a Resume Summary

  • Keep it short and professional
  • Highlight relevant skills
  • Mention career goals
  • Tailor it to the job role

Conclusion

A strong resume summary can improve your chances of getting noticed by recruiters.
