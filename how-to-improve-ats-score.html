How to Improve ATS Resume Score

Many companies use Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) to filter resumes before they reach recruiters.

Ways to Improve ATS Score

  • Use job-specific keywords
  • Choose a simple resume format
  • Avoid images and graphics
  • Use standard section headings
  • Include measurable achievements

Common ATS Mistakes

  • Using tables and columns
  • Missing important keywords
  • Submitting resumes in unsupported formats

Conclusion

An ATS-friendly resume increases your chances of passing initial screening and getting interviews.
