How to Write a Resume With No Experience

Freshers often worry about having no work experience. A strong resume can still help you get interviews.

What to Include

  • Education
  • Projects
  • Certifications
  • Internships
  • Skills
  • Volunteer Work

Resume Structure

  1. Contact Information
  2. Career Objective
  3. Education
  4. Skills
  5. Projects
  6. Certifications

Conclusion

Focus on achievements and skills rather than experience.
